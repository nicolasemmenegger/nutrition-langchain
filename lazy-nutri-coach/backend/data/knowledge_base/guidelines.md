# Practical Healthy Eating Guidelines (Non-medical)

- Aim for a balance of **protein, fiber, and healthy fats** in meals to improve satiety.
- Prefer **minimally processed foods** and **whole grains** to support micronutrient intake.
- For weight management, monitor **daily energy intake** and prioritize **protein** to maintain lean mass.
- Hydration: Spread water intake throughout the day; include 1 glass at each meal.
- Batch-cook simple staples (legumes, rice, roast veg, proteins) to reduce friction on busy days.

> This knowledge base is illustrative and not a replacement for registered dietitian advice.
